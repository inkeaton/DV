Categorical Data

This bar chart demonstrates the distribution of categorical variables. The vertical height corresponds to the magnitude of the data point.

Time Series Analysis

Line charts are optimal for tracking changes over short and long periods of time. This example shows a sine wave with added noise.

Correlation & Distribution

Scatter plots identify relationships between two variables. The radius of the circles adds a third dimension to the data.

Proportional Data

Donut charts express a "part-to-whole" relationship. Segments represent the percentage contribution of each category.
